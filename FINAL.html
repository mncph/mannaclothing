The Manifesto

Faith can
move
mountains.

— Matthew 17:20

The verse we build every stitch on. Not a slogan — a standard. If faith the size of a mustard seed can move a mountain, it can carry a mission too.

In the wilderness, the Israelites had nothing to eat for forty years. So God rained bread from heaven — manna. Not stockpiled, not hoarded. Provided daily, without fail.

That's the name on the label. A reminder, every time you get dressed, that provision isn't earned — it's given. God is our faithful Provider, and every piece we make is a small act of gratitude for that.

Provided,
not
earned.

Manna Clothing is a faith-driven mission to glorify God through what we wear — clothing as testimony, not just fabric.
